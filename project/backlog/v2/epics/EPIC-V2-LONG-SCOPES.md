# EPIC-V2-LONG-SCOPES — 5-Year + Life sheets

## Goal

Add deepest planning scopes per Reflexión doc model:

- 5-Year: vivid vision ("describe a Tuesday 5 years from today"), capabilities to build, network, financial freedom horizon, 5 anniversaries
- Life: mission (1 sentence), "soy alguien que…" statements, core values, eulogy, anti-vision, bucket list (4 buckets)

## When to atomize

After v1.5 quarter/year sheets adopted + retention strong. These are aspirational; users won't engage with them unless deeper buy-in established first.
